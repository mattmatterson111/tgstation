25 June 2020

ATHATH updated:

  • The tox purging threshold for livers has been changed from 3u to 3u * [liver's health]/[liver's maximum health].

EdgeLordExe updated:

  • Otherwordly presence has taken more control over the holes in reality.
  • Influences now actually spawn randomly on the station.

Jared-Fogle updated:

  • The search bar will now automatically focus when you open up the orbit menu, the uplink, the biogenerator, and the camera console.

Melbert updated:

  • Allows the HONK Oingo-Boingo Punch-Face to toggle between lethal and non-lethal modes.
  • The HONK Oingo-Boingo Punch-Face's throw distance now scales on how much damage it deals. By default, it's the same distance as before.
  • Pacifists can no longer use the HONK Oingo-Boingo Punch-Face to damage people.
  • Admins can now edit the damage of the HONK Oingo-Boingo Punch-Face. Honk.

WarlockD updated:

  • You can open paper ui with double shift click

dearmochi updated:

  • Fix thrown bolas (and other items) bypassing weapon/shield blocking
